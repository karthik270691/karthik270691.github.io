About Us

Innovative Software Solutions with Expert Backing.

SpineRoots Technologies is a dynamic and innovative software solutions partnership firm established in January 2024. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge technology solutions tailored for businesses across multiple domains, with a strong focus on the US healthcare service provider domain, HR solutions, IT services, Incident management, and LMS compliance products.

We believe in creating robust, scalable, and future-ready software that drives efficiency and enhances operational performance.
